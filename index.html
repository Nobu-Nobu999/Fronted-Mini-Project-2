Relic Stat

    Body: CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG
    Feet: SPD or ATK%
    Sphere: Lightning DMG or ATK%
    Rope: ATK%	
    

    Relic Sub stats
    CRIT Rate ★★★
    CRIT DMG ★★★
    SPD ★★
    ATK% ★★

Despite being a Nihility unit, Acheron works as your typical DPS who takes advantage of CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG stats. Speed boots are great for Acheron since they increase her action count, which helps to increase Slashed Dream stacks. It's also good if you're trying to hit speed breakpoints for Ornaments that require it. However, you can also use ATK% boots for Acheron if she's paired with Sparkle or Bronya.
